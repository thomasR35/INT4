    Welcome to Freshly Restaurant

    Freshly Restaurant is a family owned Mediterranean restaurant, located in the heart of New York City. Our specialities include classic American cuisine, warm, polished service and beautiful rooms suitable for a wide range of events. Dicover our menus today. We have a wide range of dishes and are proud to offer you each day freshness and diversity in the plate !

    We are open 7 days a week and we are always happy to serve you, we can welcome up to 50 guests but always have a place for you. Weither you're in a large corporate group or just a few friends visiting the big apple, we're always happy to welcome you
